## Use palette:

#### 1. At the beginning of the file where the palette is used, you need to initialize it:

        `@use "src/style/palette"`;

#### 2. After that the following syntax will be available: palette.color(code, version). Default: version-palette = 'new'.

        `palette.orange(1, "old")`
